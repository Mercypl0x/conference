UX Day Graz is a community event for practitioners of user experience, interaction design, and usability. It is our local contribution towards World Usability Day.



Wed 06 Nov 2024 through Sat 09 Nov 2024 from 08:00-17:00
Lecture Theatre i13, Graz University of Technology,
Inffeldgasse 16b, Basement, 8010 Graz

http://uxdaygraz.org/
https://uxdaygraz2017.isds.tugraz.at/

is free to attend, but advance registration is required, and the number of places is limited. The event is a non-smoking event and the language of the event is English.

Guest accounts for the university’s wifi will be available at registration. For users of twitter, we suggest using the hashtag #uxg17 when referring to the event.

Slides and videos are linked from the Programme page.

UX Graz - The Graz UX Community

The UX Graz community hold a meetup every 3 months or so. Come along or join the mailing list to stay informed.
