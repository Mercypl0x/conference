Description:

The Inffeldgasse Campus is the largest of the three TU Graz campus sites. Currently covering an area of around 124,000 square meters. The event takes place within the lecture halls located at this campus of Graz University of Technology.

Directions:

Going west in Inffeldgasse, turn right at the bike stalls next to building number 10. Walk around this building, then turn left. Keep walking straight until you see building number 16b on your right. Head inside, down the stairs to your right, where you’ll find all the relevant lecture halls.
